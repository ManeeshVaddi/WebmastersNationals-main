What You Can Do..

Saving Earth is Teamwork and Everyone needs your help as well. The little change in everyday thing you do makes the biggest changes in society as a whole. For example, switching your bulbs to LED is also a way to positively impact the environment as they use 75% less energy than other bulbs like the incandescant ones.

Everything starts out small like a small act of kindness. So, begin your clean energy journey with something small and look at the impact that you might cause.
